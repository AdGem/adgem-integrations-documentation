Payment Guide

Getting Paid​

Payment Schedule​

Payment Schedule: Net 60

  • Payments are paid on a Net 60 payment term from the end of the month.
  • Example: If your earnings thresholds are met for August, you will be paid August earnings on November 1st.

Payout Methods​

Supported payout methods:

  • Bank Transfer
  • PayPal

Minimum Payment Terms​

  • Minimum payment for ACH/PayPal is $100.00
  • Minimum payment for Wire is $500.00

Historical Payments​

To view all historical payments, click on the History tab.

Missing Payments​

If you have met your payment terms and did not receive your payments, or you are experiencing payment issues, please reach out to your account manager for assistance.
