# Third-party Integrations

If you reward players through a **loyalty or rewards platform** — such as **Paytronix** or **PDI** — AdGem conversions can be delivered through that platform's integration rather than through your own postback endpoint. The platform receives the reward events from AdGem and credits your players according to your program.

To set this up, contact your **Publisher Support Advocate**. They'll confirm whether your platform is supported and configure the integration for your account.

If you control reward delivery yourself instead, use [server postbacks](/docs/integrate/reward-mechanism/postbacks-v3) or [client-side polling](/docs/integrate/reward-mechanism/client-polling).
