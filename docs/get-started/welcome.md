# Welcome to AdGem

AdGem provides publishers with premium monetization solutions designed to maximize revenue while delivering great user experiences.

## Why AdGem?

- **Premium demand** — Reach captivated audiences worldwide to maximize your ARPDAU and eCPMs with the industry's top advertisers.
- **Earn more** — Monetize your user base and increase revenue when players engage with AdGem's high-performing ad units.
- **Direct access to the experts** — Dedicated, in-house expertise and live chat for monetization efficiencies, including best-in-class documentation.
- **Up-to-the-minute reporting** — Real-time analytics provide complete transparency with immediate, detailed results.

## Choose your integration

AdGem delivers offers two ways — **pre-built** (we provide the UI) or **partner-built** (you build your own):

**Pre-built (fastest)**

- [Web Offerwall](/docs/integrate/offer-delivery/pre-built/web-offerwall)
- [iOS SDK](/docs/integrate/offer-delivery/pre-built/ios-sdk)
- [Android SDK](/docs/integrate/offer-delivery/pre-built/android-sdk)
- [Unity SDK](/docs/integrate/offer-delivery/pre-built/unity-sdk)

**Partner-built (custom experience)**

- [Prism (GraphQL)](/docs/integrate/offer-delivery/partner-built/prism)
- [Offer API (REST)](/docs/integrate/offer-delivery/partner-built/offer-api)

For full API specs, see the [Reference](/docs/reference) section (Prism, Offer API, Reporting API).
