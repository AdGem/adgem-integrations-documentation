# Ad Units

## Offerwall

The AdGem Offer Wall displays high-performing and high-paying mobile ad campaigns that users can complete to earn in-game virtual currency, points and content. With the AdGem Offer Wall, as a publisher, you can expect high fill rates around the world with ultra-competitive payouts for all offers. This engaging ad format is a battle-tested ad unit that is perfect for the mobile gaming experience across almost all gaming genres as well as in utility and tool-based apps.

We support most of the major platforms currently and plan to support additional platforms in the future. If there is a platform you would like to see us support, please let us know!
