# Developer Support

Need help with your AdGem integration? There are a couple of ways to reach us.

## Get in touch

- **Your Publisher Support Advocate** — every publisher has a dedicated advocate. They're your fastest path for integration questions, go-live coordination, and account-specific issues.
- **[Contact AdGem](https://adgem.com/contact/)** — for general inquiries, or if you don't yet have an advocate.

## Common topics

- **Setting up your app** — see the [Quickstart](/docs/get-started/quickstart).
- **Choosing an integration** — see [Decide your path](/docs/get-started/decide).
- **Reward delivery and postbacks** — see the [Reward mechanism guide](/docs/integrate/reward-mechanism).

## Missing rewards for a completed offer?

Player-facing reward issues are handled by AdGem Player Support. See the [Player Support Overview](/docs/resources/player-support-overview) for how that process works and what's expected of publishers.
