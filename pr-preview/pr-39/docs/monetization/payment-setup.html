Payment Setup

If you experience any issues with adding your payout methods or completing your tax forms, please reach out to your account manager for assistance. All forms must be completed in order for you to receive your payment.

Payment Details Page​

How to access the Payment Details page:

  1. Sign in to the AdGem platform.
  2. In the left navigation pane, select Payment > Payment Details.

Address Information​

How to view, edit, or add address information:

  1. Click on the Payment tab.
  2. To edit your existing information, click the Edit button located at the bottom right of the form.
    • If you have never submitted any address information, you will see a blank address form.
  3. Complete the form.

Address Form​

Payment Method​

How to view, edit, or add payment method information.

note

You will not be able to access the Payment Method tab until you have completed the Address form.

  1. To edit your existing information, click the Edit button located at the bottom right of the form.
  2. Select your payment method.
  3. Complete the form.

Payment Method Form​

Tax Forms​

How to view, edit, or add tax forms.

note

You will not be able to access the Tax Form tab until you have completed the Address and Payment Method forms.

  1. To view your existing information or to submit a new tax form, click the corresponding hyperlink.
  2. Select your tax form.
  3. Complete the form.
caution

You cannot edit an existing tax form. You will need to submit a new form.

Tax Form​

tip

For details on payment schedules, payout methods, and minimum thresholds, see the Payment Guide.
